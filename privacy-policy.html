Privacy Policy

PKT Precision App
Effective date: April 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PKT Precision collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app to connect to and tune compatible devices over Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE).

1) Information We May Access

  • Bluetooth connection data, such as discovered devices and connection status
  • Configuration values and tuning parameters read from or written to your device
  • Configuration files you choose to import or export on your local device

2) What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell personal data to third parties
  • We do not collect location data for user tracking
  • We do not request unrelated data beyond app functionality needs

3) How Information Is Used

  • To connect to compatible PKT devices via BLE
  • To read/write tuning values and display real-time results
  • To import/export configuration files when requested by the user

4) Permissions Used by the App

  • Bluetooth: used to discover and connect to devices
  • File access: used to import/export configuration files selected by the user

5) Data Storage and Security

Configuration data and related usage data are stored on your device as part of app operation. We apply reasonable measures to protect information against unauthorized access.

6) Third-Party Services

The app may rely on platform libraries or system services to provide core functionality. Those services may have their own privacy policies.

7) Children’s Privacy

This app is not specifically intended for children under 13.

8) Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date on this page will be updated accordingly.
